Why Sri Lanka?

Sri Lanka offers a unique blend of tropical beauty, ancient history, diverse wildlife and warm hospitality. From Sigiriya to Ella and Mirissa to Jaffna, every corner tells a story.

Must Visit Destinations

Sigiriya

Ancient rock fortress and UNESCO World Heritage site.

Ella

Tea plantations, misty mountains and iconic train journeys.

Mirissa

Whale watching, palm-fringed beaches and sunset cruises.

Kandy

Home of the sacred Temple of the Tooth and hill city charm.

Yala

One of Asia's best wildlife parks for leopards and elephants.
